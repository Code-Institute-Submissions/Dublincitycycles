Dublin City Cycle Routes

We offer a variety of spectacular routes from our base in Dublin 3. Whether you are looking for an urban ride, a coastal scenic route or a step back in time to an ancient castle, we have something for you! All of our tours are offered on a 'one-way' basis. Each and every one of our tours ends within a 5min walk to a train station and several bus routes to bring you back to the city, however, we do encourage our guests to take some time and explore the areas in which you finish the tours, as each destination is uniquely beautiful. Each tour lasts approx. 2.5 hrs and includes; Bikes, Helmets, Bike Accesories, Guided tour and your Dublin City Cycles 'pack'.

Howth Summit Coastal Route

This beautiful coastal route takes you from the outskirts of the city along a 10KM strech of coastal paved cycle track to the base of Howth hill. From here, you will have a sharp ascent to howth summit, taking in spectacular views of Dublin bay and a panoramic view of Dublin from the summit. Howth boasts stunning cliff walks, lush wildlife and a fantastic selection of award winning restaraunts and pubs.
This route is approximately 20km with sharp incline.

Difficuly level intermediate to advanced

Malahide Castle Coastal Route

The Malahide Castle Coastal Route takes you from the outskirts of the city along an 8KM strech of coastal paved cycle track. Along the way you will take in sights of Bull Island, Dollymount Beach and St. Annes park, before turning towards Malahide. From here, you will enter a second, newly constructed cycle path that offers views of the famous Portmarnock Golf Club and stunning scenery of North County Dublin. A further 4KM strecth will take you along the edge of the Irish sea and Portmarnock beach, boasting stunning views of Ireland' eye and Lambay Island before arriving in the picturesque village of Malahide. From here, it is a short cycle to the 12th century Malahide Castle and dememse.
This route is mostly flat and approx. 20km

Difficuly level beginner to intermediate

Dublin City

The Dublin City Tour takes you right through the heart of the City, while taking in some of the most famous sites. Along the way you will visit; the GPO, O'Connell Street, Dublin's Golden Whiskey Triangle, The Guinness Brewery, Ireland's oldest pub, Dublin Castle and Grafton Street. This unique way of seeing Dublin gives you a great perspective on how to get around the City and what to do during your stay in Ireland. This route is mostly flat and approx 25km.

Difficuly level easy

Dun Laioghre Pier

The Dun Laoighre tour takes you from Dublin City to the famous harbour town of Dun Laioghre. Marvel at the spectacular georgian architecture, Sea views and picturesque viallges as you meander your way through South Dublin. Dun Laioghre town has something for everyone, be it a coastal cruise or a dinner with a view. This route is mostly flat and approximately 15km

Difficuly level easy
