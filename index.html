Dublin City Cycles

Explore Dublin with Dublin City Cycles and take in all of the wonderful History, Architecture, Culture and Scenery that Dublin has to offer - on two wheels! Choose from beautiful coastal routes to urban, city cycles through Dublin's beautiful historic streets. You will be guided by our professional, knowledgeable tour guides who are on hand to share stories and information and answer any questions you may have. All guests will be provided with high quality road or hyrbid bikes, helmets and a 'Dublin City Cycles' pack. Exlpore Dublin in unique way and get off the beaten track with us. Whether you're looking a city cycle, a coastal trip or a a visit to an ancient castle, we have the trip for you!

Our Bikes

All of our bikes come with helmets, locks, lights and bells.

Road Bikes

Our professional grade road bikes are perfect for paved cycle tracks and on-road cycling at speed. These high quality bikes are well suited to the more serious biker and come with a minimum of 27 Gears giving you everything you need to tackle hills.

Hybrid Bikes

Our hybrid bikes again offer all of the speed of a road-bike, with slightly larger wheels for added comfort and straight handlebars for a more comfortable cruising position. We also offer womens frames on our hybrids.

City bikes

Our city bikes are perfect for cruising lesiruely through Dublins city streets. These bikes come with larger wheels, a lower frame and wider handlebars to offer the rider the most comfortable seat while crusing the beautiful city streets
